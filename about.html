About Us

Your trusted partner for quality German Shepherds in Kenya.

Our Story

GuardianPaws Kennels began as a passion project dedicated to the care and preservation of the noble German Shepherd breed. Based in the serene environment of Ruiru, Kenya, we have grown into a trusted name, known for our commitment to producing healthy, intelligent, and well-tempered German Shepherds. With years of experience, we understand the importance of matching the right dog to the right family, and we take pride in our personalized approach.

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise German Shepherds that excel as companions, working dogs, and guardians. We are dedicated to maintaining the breed's integrity, promoting responsible breeding practices, and providing unmatched care for every puppy we nurture.

Why Choose GuardianPaws Kennels?

Health & Care

We prioritize the health and well-being of our dogs, providing regular veterinary checkups and a balanced diet.

Expertise

With years of experience, we understand German Shepherds inside out and ensure they reach their full potential.

Training Programs

From basic obedience to advanced security training, we help shape versatile and well-disciplined dogs.

Post-Sale Support

Our relationship doesn’t end after purchase; we provide guidance and support to ensure a seamless transition.
